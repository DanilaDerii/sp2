SP2 Notes
=========

Last audited: 2026-06-30

- SQLite cannot enforce a real foreign key into LanceDB.
- The relationship between SQLite `installed_packs.id` and LanceDB
  `pack_chunks.installed_pack_id` is enforced by application code.
- Use `installed_pack_id` as the local retrieval scope.
- Keep retrieval/search logic out of low-level CRUD modules.
- Docling is too cumbersome for simple text-layer PDFs; keep the fast
  `pdftotext` path and revisit Docling only for scanned or complex files.
- Keep this notes file short. Durable behavior belongs in `progress.txt` and
  schema details belong in `data_schema.txt`.
